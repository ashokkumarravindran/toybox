Overview
Toybox is an AI-powered design repository and showcase platform created to help teams organize, discover, understand, and reuse valuable experience design artifacts.
Design organizations generate thousands of assets across strategy, research, UX, CX, service design, product design, marketing, and innovation initiatives. These artifacts often become fragmented across multiple tools, repositories, shared drives, presentations, and documents, making them difficult to discover, understand, and leverage over time.
Toybox reimagines the design repository as an intelligent experience layer that not only stores assets, but also transforms them into rich, curated showcases that communicate project context, outcomes, decisions, and business value.
⸻
Problem Statement
Organizations invest significant time and resources creating design artifacts, yet much of that knowledge becomes difficult to access once projects are completed.
Common challenges include:
	●	Assets scattered across multiple platforms and file systems
	●	Limited visibility into project outcomes and decision-making
	●	Inconsistent documentation standards
	●	Difficulty onboarding new team members to past work
	●	Valuable knowledge becoming inaccessible over time
	●	Lack of a compelling way to showcase design impact
Traditional repositories focus on storage. Teams need a system that focuses on understanding, storytelling, discovery, and reuse.
⸻
Design Vision
Toybox aims to become an intelligent design knowledge platform that can ingest project artifacts from multiple sources and automatically generate curated project showcases.
The long-term vision is to create a centralized experience layer that:
	●	Connects to design and collaboration tools
	●	Understands project context through AI
	●	Generates showcase-ready case studies
	●	Identifies patterns across projects
	●	Surfaces relevant work based on user needs
	●	Preserves institutional knowledge
	●	Enables teams to discover and learn from past work
Rather than acting as a file repository, Toybox serves as a living knowledge system for experience teams.
