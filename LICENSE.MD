Toybox was originally conceived and designed by Ashok Kumar Ravindran, Senior Consultant, Experience Design.
This repository contains concepts, documentation, prototypes, and implementation artifacts related to the Toybox platform.
The Toybox concept, experience vision, and roadmap were created to explore how organizational knowledge can be transformed into Curated Intelligence through AI-powered discovery, storytelling, and experience acceleration.
Contributors are encouraged to collaborate, enhance, and evolve the platform. Significant strategic enhancements or extensions should, where practical, be discussed with the original creator to maintain alignment with the broader vision and experience principles that guide the platform.
This notice is intended to acknowledge authorship of the original concept and encourage collaborative stewardship of the platform as it evolves.
